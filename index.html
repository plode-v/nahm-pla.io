Our Story

A taste of
Thailand
in D.C.

Nahm Pla, meaning "fish sauce" in Thai is the soul of every great Thai dish. Humble but essential. That's exactly who we are: a neighbourhood restaurant grounded in the authentic flavours of Thailand.

We bring the bold curries, bright salads, and fragrant noodle soups that Thai families have shared for generations, made fresh every day in our kitchen.
